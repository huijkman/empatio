About People

What is Accessibility? Simply put it is Usability. It is helping people from A to B in the most efficient and friendly way. And to do so you need to know who these people are. It al starts with understanding who your users are. And all users have different needs. Here are some stories about people to get you going
The videos are in dutch and have english captions

About Empat.io
