What is Accessibility?

If you don’t know who your are users then it's difficult to design or develop for them. Accessibility is about people, your users. Empat.io shows who they are and what you can do to help them. By letting them tell you what does and what doesn’t work for them in web and app.
The videos are in dutch and have english captions
