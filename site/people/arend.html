How can you help Arend?

For designers

Remove distraction, keep the essential. People who depend on spoken texts only don't have a visual overview of a webpage or an app. By removing unnecessary information and focus on important information you can help that person accomplish their task.

  • 3 steps to achieving simplicity
  • Examples about how to reduce friction in your design

For developers

Develop screenreader friendly. Make users with a screenreader happy by building extra assistence and shortcuts for them. Also experience the use of your website/app with a screenreader.

  • How to add extra assistence for a screenreader in your website/app (Android | iOS | Web | Windows Phone)
