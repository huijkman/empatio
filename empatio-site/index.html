What is it like to live with a handicap? What is it like to be hindered when using an app or website? To answer these questions I have started creating short movies about people. These are the first stories.
The videos are in dutch and have english captions

About Accessibility

These videos were created mainly out of discontent with the way accessibility is being addressed in web-projects. Most of the time it is seen as a technical issue that has to be solved with technical solutions. But that is only part of the story. When I started testing with real people, with real handicaps, it became all too clear that only understanding the technical part just isn't enough. It is also about usability and a thorough understanding of the needs of your users.
